As a resident of Brooklyn Heights, my favorite place to spend my free time is Brooklyn Bridge Park. This park makes excellent use of a relatively small space to offer New Yorkers a place to interact with native flora and fauna. It is also home to some of the most striking views of the city, its rivers, and its bridges.
